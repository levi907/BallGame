How to Play

Goal: Survive 30 turns without going bankrupt!

Each turn: Pay the pull cost to draw a random ball from your machine. The ball gives you cash, tickets, or special effects.

Cash ($): Used to pay for pulls. If you can't afford a pull, you lose!

Tickets (tx): Used to buy new balls and upgrades from the shop.

Pull cost: Increases every 5 turns (new round). Plan ahead!

Shop: Refreshes every round with 3 balls and 3 upgrades. Available after pulling.

Strategy: Remove weak starter balls, add stronger ones, and build combos to stay alive!
