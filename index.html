How to Play

Goal: Survive 30 turns without going bankrupt!

Each turn: Pay the pull cost, then draw a random ball. Balls give you $ cash or ★ tickets.

After pulling, draft a new ball to add to your machine.

$ Cash pays for pulls. Can't afford the turn cost? You lose.

★ Tickets buy relics (passive effects) and upgrades from the shop.

Pull cost increases every 5 turns (rounds). Plan ahead!

Strategy: Draft strong balls, buy relics for combos, remove weak starters!
