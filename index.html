How to Play

Goal: Survive 30 turns without going bankrupt!

Each turn: Pay the pull cost to draw a random ball. The ball gives you $ cash or ★ tickets.

$ Cash pays for pulls. Can't afford one? You lose.

★ Tickets buy new balls and upgrades from the shop.

Pull cost increases every 5 turns. Plan ahead!

Shop refreshes each round with 3 balls and 3 upgrades.

Strategy: Remove weak starter balls, add stronger ones, build combos!
